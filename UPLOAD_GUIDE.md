# UPLOAD_GUIDE.md

## Instructions for Uploading Trained LoRA Model to SeaArt.ai, Pixai.art, and Civitai

This guide provides detailed steps for successfully uploading your trained LoRA model to the platforms SeaArt.ai, Pixai.art, and Civitai.

### 1. Account Setup
#### a. SeaArt.ai
1. Visit the [SeaArt.ai website](https://www.seaart.ai).
2. Click on the 'Sign Up' button.
3. Fill in the required details like email, username, and password.
4. Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to your inbox.

#### b. Pixai.art
1. Navigate to [Pixai.art](https://www.pixai.art).
2. Create an account by clicking on 'Join' or 'Sign Up'.
3. Complete the registration form and submit.
4. Check your email for a verification link and confirm your account.

#### c. Civitai
1. Go to the [Civitai website](https://civitai.com).
2. Click on 'Register' in the top right corner.
3. Enter the necessary information and register.
4. Look for a verification email and follow the instructions to verify your account.

### 2. Metadata Preparation
- Prepare the following metadata to include with your upload:
  - Title of the model
  - Description of the model (features, training data, etc.)
  - Tags relevant to your model (e.g., AI, LoRA, ML, etc.)
  - Any images or examples of model outputs (optional, but recommended)

### 3. Upload Steps
#### a. SeaArt.ai
1. Log in to your SeaArt.ai account.
2. Navigate to the 'Upload Model' section.
3. Fill out the upload form with the prepared metadata.
4. Select the trained LoRA model file to upload.
5. Review your information and click 'Submit'.

#### b. Pixai.art
1. Sign in to your Pixai.art account.
2. Go to the 'Upload' page.
3. Complete the required metadata fields with your model information.
4. Upload the LoRA model file from your computer.
5. Double-check all information, then hit 'Upload'.

#### c. Civitai
1. Access your Civitai account.
2. Click on 'Submit Model'.
3. Provide the necessary details in the submission form.
4. Upload the trained LoRA model.
5. Confirm your submission by clicking 'Finish'.

### 4. Verification Steps
- After uploading, monitor your email for any communications regarding your model submission.
- Check the respective platform’s dashboard or profile page to ensure your model appears as expected.
- If there are any issues, follow up with the support teams of the respective platforms.

### Conclusion
By following these steps, you should be able to successfully upload your trained LoRA model to SeaArt.ai, Pixai.art, and Civitai.
